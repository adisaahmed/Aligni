Office 365 & Microsoft Dynamics | Making your Business More Collaborative

When it comes to Business successes, it is crucial to deal with Collaboration and Communication Efficiency! Far from being a hassle, it is now very easy to work smarter. With Office 365 Business Applications such as Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook, re-structure the way your employees communicate and collaborate.

By adopting a Cloud Technology such as Microsoft Office 365, you allow your business to drive growth, improve security and optimize costs. That is why, in 2020, more than 78% of Small and Medium Businesses' IT environment will be hosted in the Cloud.

Office 365 Increases Business Productivity

Office 365 working with your other Cloud-based Applications, give your business more flexibility. Give your employees the opportunities to securely access and edit documents on-the-go and in real-time. Many Businesses take advantage of the convergence of cloud solutions and mobile technology to ensure productivity, accommodate different work styles, and reduce costs. The cloud also provides high-level security and privacy while ensuring the access control you need.

O365 Includes Power BI for Valuable Business Insights

Power BI is a suite of business analytics tools that deliver insights throughout your organization. Connect to hundreds of data sources, simplify data prep, and drive ad hoc analysis. Produce beautiful reports, then publish them for your organization to consume on the web and across mobile devices. Everyone can create personalized dashboards with a unique, 360-degree view of their business.

With New Treemap and waterfall charts in Office 365, it is now very easy to visualize big amount of data, and to publish them directly into Microsoft Power BI from Excel. By using these new tools, your business can connect, view, and shape data from multiple sources, including tables from websites, unstructured sources, and any outside data service.

Office 365 for MS Dynamics 365

Microsoft Office 365 for Microsoft Dynamics ERP system combines the business tools you're already familiar in the Cloud, along with enabling the next-generation of communication and collaboration tools.

This creates Modular, Modern Business Applications using a "Common Data Model".

Microsoft Office 365 & Microsoft Dynamics is not only user-friendly, it's totally secure being backed by the trusted cloud of Microsoft Azure.
